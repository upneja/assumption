## POSTING STRATEGY

### Pre-Launch (2 weeks before)
- Post 2-3 behind-the-scenes videos
- Build anticipation with "coming soon" messaging
- Tease features without revealing too much

### Launch Week
- Day 1-2: Problem/solution videos (prime the audience)
- Day 3-4: "We just launched!" + beta testing reaction videos
- Day 5-7: Viral hook videos (maximize reach)

### Post-Launch
- Mix of user-generated content, reactions, and development updates
- Respond to every comment in first hour
- Duet/stitch popular videos in your niche

### Hashtag Strategy
- 3-5 niche hashtags (#pregame, #partygames)
- 3-5 medium hashtags (#college, #party)
- 2-3 broad hashtags (#viral, #funny)

### Best Posting Times (College Demographic)
- 7-9am: Before class scroll
- 12-2pm: Lunch break scroll
- 6-8pm: Dinner scroll
- 10pm-12am: Night scroll (BEST engagement)
- Thursday-Saturday: Party planning mode

### Content Mix
- 40% Educational/Behind-the-scenes (builds credibility)
- 30% Entertaining/Reactions (builds virality)
- 30% Value/Solutions (builds conversions)

### Response Templates for Common Comments
- "What's the app called?" → "pregame.lol - link in bio!"
- "Is it free?" → "Yep! Base game free forever"
- "iOS or Android?" → "Your friends join from browser - works on any phone"
- "How many people?" → "4-12 people, perfect for pregames"
- "This is just like [competitor]" → "Try the browser join feature - game changer"
