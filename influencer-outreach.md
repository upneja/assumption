# Influencer Outreach DM Templates for pregame.lol

## Overview
These templates are designed for TikTok creators in the college/party/gaming space. The approach is collaboration-focused, not transactional. We're offering early access and a unique filming opportunity, not asking for free promotion.

---

## INFLUENCER TARGETING CRITERIA

### Primary Targets (High Priority)
- **Follower Count:** 10k-100k (big enough to matter, small enough to respond)
- **Engagement Rate:** 5%+ (likes + comments / followers)
- **Content Type:** College life, party content, friend group videos
- **Posting Frequency:** 3+ times per week (active creators)
- **Comment Section:** Creator actually responds to comments (shows they're engaged)

### Red Flags (Skip These)
- Only posts sponsored content (won't feel authentic)
- Engagement seems bought (10k followers, 50 likes per video)
- Totally different audience (family vloggers, etc.)
- Haven't posted in 30+ days (inactive)

### Ideal Creator Profile
"College student or recent grad who makes videos with their friend group. Regular party/pregame content. Audience is 18-24. Good engagement. Responds to DMs. Not oversaturated with sponsorships."

## OUTREACH STRATEGY

### Phase 1: Micro-Influencers (10k-50k followers)
- Easier to get responses
- More authentic
- Better engagement rates
- Send 10 DMs per day

### Phase 2: Mid-Tier (50k-100k followers)
- After you have some testimonials/proof
- Can reference smaller creators who tried it
- Send 5 DMs per day

### Phase 3: Macro (100k+ followers)
- Only after product-market fit proven
- Need social proof from smaller creators
- Very selective (2-3 perfect-fit creators)

### Timing
- **Best Days:** Thursday-Saturday (weekend planning mode)
- **Best Time:** 6-9pm (after school/work, before going out)
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, late nights

### Response Rate Expectations
- 10-20% will open your DM
- 5-10% will respond
- 2-5% will actually try it
- 1-2% will post about it

**Math:** Send 100 DMs ‚Üí 5 responses ‚Üí 2 try it ‚Üí 1 posts about it

**Q: "Do I have to post about it?"**

A: "Early access to all features, custom question packs for your vibe, and if you do post, a promo code so you can track your impact. Plus if this thing takes off, I'm down to discuss rev share for creators who bring a lot of users."

## SUCCESS METRICS

Track these to know if influencer outreach is working:

- **Response Rate:** % of DMs that get a response
- **Try Rate:** % of responses that actually try the product
- **Post Rate:** % of people who try it that post about it
- **Traffic:** Users who came from each creator's code
- **Engagement:** Do users from influencers stick around?

**Goal Metrics:**
- 10% response rate (10 responses per 100 DMs)
- 20% try rate (2 try it per 10 responses)
- 50% post rate (1 posts per 2 who try)
- 100 DMs ‚Üí 10 responses ‚Üí 2 try it ‚Üí 1 posts ‚Üí 50+ signups

Adjust strategy based on what's working!
